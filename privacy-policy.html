Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Prime Risk Advisory respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our responsible data practices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone, and organization details submitted via secure inquiry forms
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, device information, and usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and referral sources for analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide tailored advisory services
  • Improve website functionality, performance, and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements
  • Send important service updates (with opt-out option)

3. Data Protection Measures

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all form submissions
  • Secure cloud hosting with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Data anonymization where possible
  • Employee training on data protection best practices

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Data sharing occurs only in these limited cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • Service providers under strict confidentiality agreements (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal requirements or government requests
  • Business transfers (mergers, acquisitions)

5. Data Retention

Inquiry data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill your request (typically 12 months). Technical logs are kept for 90 days for security purposes.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications

7. Cookies & Tracking

This website uses essential cookies for functionality and Google Analytics (anonymized). You can manage preferences via browser settings.
